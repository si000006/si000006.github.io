OC/sona building

OC stands for original characters. And Sona means an artistic representation of yourself. Which is what the above image is, however, I got more than one Sona. Building them is writing stories, building a world they live in. Make your own magical land or a sci-fi future can be really fun. Sometimes commissioning artists to draw illustrations for them. This part is the main way I spend my hobby money.

Plamo Painting

Plamo means plasic models. I am interested mainly in sci fi models like giant robots or futuristic vehicle. Very intrigued by how painting can change the look and feel of a model. Not just by changing colors, but also adding volume, lighting, different textures to it. It's kind of like drawing, but on a three dimensional shape.
